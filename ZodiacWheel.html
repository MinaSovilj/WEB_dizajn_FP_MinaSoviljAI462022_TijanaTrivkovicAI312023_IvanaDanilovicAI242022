    • The Zodiac Wheel

    The Zodiac Wheel represents the continuous journey of the Sun through the twelve signs of the zodiac. Each section of the circle reveals a unique energy, personality, and lesson that contributes to the story of human experience.

    Beginning with bold Aries and ending with dreamy Pisces, the wheel symbolizes the cycle of growth — from self-discovery to spiritual completion. Every sign builds upon the previous one, creating a cosmic rhythm that mirrors life itself.

    The Twelve Zodiac Signs

    • Aries ♈: Courage, leadership, and fiery beginnings.
    • Taurus ♉: Stability, sensuality, and appreciation for beauty.
    • Gemini ♊: Curiosity, communication, and mental agility.
    • Cancer ♋: Emotion, intuition, and nurturing energy.
    • Leo ♌: Confidence, creativity, and self-expression.
    • Virgo ♍: Precision, logic, and service to others.
    • Libra ♎: Harmony, balance, and the art of relationships.
    • Scorpio ♏: Transformation, depth, and emotional power.
    • Sagittarius ♐: Exploration, wisdom, and spiritual truth.
    • Capricorn ♑: Discipline, ambition, and long-term goals.
    • Aquarius ♒: Innovation, independence, and collective vision.
    • Pisces ♓: Compassion, dreams, and connection to the divine.

    Zodiac Elements

    Fire Signs

    Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius — driven by passion, creativity, and bold action.

    Earth Signs

    Taurus, Virgo, and Capricorn — grounded, practical, and dependable energies.

    Air Signs

    Gemini, Libra, and Aquarius — intellectual, communicative, and idealistic forces.

    Water Signs

    Cancer, Scorpio, and Pisces — intuitive, emotional, and deeply sensitive souls.
