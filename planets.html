    • Planets and Their Influence

    In astrology, planets are more than just celestial bodies—they symbolize different types of energy that influence our lives. Each planet governs specific aspects of experience, guiding the way we think, feel, and act.

    The Sun represents vitality and core identity, providing the energy that drives us forward. The Moon governs emotions, instincts, and inner life, shaping how we respond to the world on a personal level.

    Mercury influences communication, thought processes, and learning, while Venus rules love, beauty, and harmony, reflecting how we connect with others. Mars represents drive, ambition, and assertiveness, encouraging action and initiative.

    Outer planets such as Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, and Pluto impact broader life themes, growth, challenges, and transformation. Understanding planetary positions in a natal chart helps reveal how these energies interact and shape life experiences.

    Planet Overview

    Sun

    Represents identity, vitality, and life purpose.

    Moon

    Governs emotions, instincts, and subconscious patterns.

    Mercury

    Influences communication, intellect, and learning style.

    Venus

    Rules love, relationships, and appreciation of beauty.

    Mars

    Represents drive, courage, and physical energy.

    Jupiter

    Associated with growth, optimism, and expansion.

    Saturn

    Represents discipline, responsibility, structure, and life lessons.

    Uranus

    Symbolizes innovation, change, rebellion, and unexpected events.

    Neptune

    Connects to intuition, dreams, imagination, and spiritual insight.

    Pluto

    Represents transformation, power, regeneration, and deep change.
